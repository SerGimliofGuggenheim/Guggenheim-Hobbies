Kingdom

Kingdom is a japanese animation set during the china's warring era
and it features the struggle and war for survival and dominance among the
seven remaining kingdoms. The story is centered around a war orphan Xin,
who aspires to become the greatest General in the middle kingdom through
aiding his best friend and king of Chin Ying Zheng, in uniting china therefore bringing an end to the 500 year old war.

The show features lots of great characters and bone chilling,
epic battle scenes.
